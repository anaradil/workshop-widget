Fedora Hubs widget tutorial
===========================

In this workshop, you will learn how to write a widget for `Fedora Hubs`_, and
how to take advantage of the automatic caching and reloading features that the
Hubs framework provides.

.. _Fedora Hubs: https://pagure.io/fedora-hubs/

Overall, the workshop takes around an hour to do, depending on your experience
and your willingness to explore alternative ways of implementing some
exercises.
